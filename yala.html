About Yala National Park

Yala National Park is the most visited and second-largest national park in Sri Lanka. It is renowned for its diverse ecosystems and wildlife, including leopards, elephants, and a variety of bird species.

Wildlife at Yala

The park is home to a rich array of wildlife, making it a popular destination for safari enthusiasts and nature lovers. Visitors can witness the majestic elephants, elusive leopards, and numerous bird species.
